In March 2025, I visited Dubai with some friends as we are all clients of the same personal trainer - [Louise Frances](https://www.instagram.com/_francesfitness/).
Lou lives in Dubai and held a client photoshoot for us to show off our progress.
This involved weeks of gruelling prep, hard hours in the gym, low calories, and a high step count to make sure we were looking at our absolute best and felt as confident as we possibly could in our chosen outfits.

This kind of training, by the way, is not a sustainable way to lose weight!
